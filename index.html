How Does It Work?

Users learn about programming through our predefined lessons, designed to ensure that our students learn to their best abilities.

Through our online community, users are able to deploy their code for other users of our platform to see! Additionally, users can fork existing programs.

Users' projects will be publically accessible to anyone across the globe! Anyone can see, critique, and learn from projects.
